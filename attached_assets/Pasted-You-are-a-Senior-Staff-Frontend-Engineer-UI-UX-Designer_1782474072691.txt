You are a Senior Staff Frontend Engineer, UI/UX Designer, Performance Engineer, and Accessibility Expert. Your task is to build a **production-ready, premium SaaS landing page** for a fictional AI-powered workflow automation platform called **FlowAI**.

**Product Name:** FlowAI

**Tagline:** *Automate your business workflows with intelligent AI.*

**Product Description:**
FlowAI is an AI-powered workflow automation platform that helps businesses automate repetitive processes, process documents with AI, integrate data from hundreds of services, generate intelligent insights, monitor workflows in real time, and collaborate seamlessly across teams.

## 5. Pricing Section

Create three pricing plans:

### Starter

### Professional

### Enterprise

Include:

* Monthly / Annual billing toggle
* Currency selector

Currencies:

* USD
* INR
* EUR

Pricing must come from a multidimensional configuration object.

Example:

pricing = {
USD:{
starter:29,
professional:79,
enterprise:199
},
INR:{
starter:1999,
professional:5999,
enterprise:14999
},
EUR:{
starter:25,
professional:69,
enterprise:179
}
}

Annual pricing MUST NOT be hardcoded.

Always compute dynamically using:

annual = monthly × 12 × 0.8

Changing currency or billing should update ONLY the displayed price.

Avoid unnecessary parent component re-renders.

Use memoization wherever appropriate.
